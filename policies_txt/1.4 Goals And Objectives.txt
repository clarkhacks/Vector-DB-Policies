GOALS AND OBJECTIVES
I. PURPOSE
The purpose of this general order is to establish a procedure for the formulation, annual
updating and distribution of the goals and objectives for the Olivette Police Department.
II. PREPARATION
A. The responsibility for the preparation of goals and objectives for the
Department rests with the Chief of Police. The responsibility for the preparation
of goals and objectives of each organizational component of the Department rests
with the commanders of those components. If a strategy or statement of mission
is to be successful, it must meet the following test:
1. The mission, or goals and objectives, must be capable of attracting
continued support from political and legal officials who authorize the
continuation of the enterprise.
2. It must be operationally feasible and should take advantage of the
distinctive competencies and capabilities of the department; and
3. It must be considered valuable to the community.
B. Goals and objectives will be updated annually for the Department.
III. DISTRIBUTION
Any updated or modified version of the Department's goals and objectives will be made
available to all agency personnel through PowerDMS.

IV. PROGRESS REPORTS
Progress made towards the attainment of Departmental goals and objectives for each
organizational component will be presented to and reviewed by the Chief of Police
annually.
