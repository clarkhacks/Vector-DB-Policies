COMMUNICATION
I. PURPOSE:
The purpose of this general order is to establish procedures for the coordination of patrol
with other department functions.
II. PROCEDURE:
All officers must cooperate and exchange information with detectives and personnel of
other functional areas of the Department. This cooperation and exchange is accomplished
by, but not limited to:
1. A Command Staff Meeting held weekly, when available.
2. Attendance of all police personnel is required at mandatory staff meetings.
3. Daily review of incidents and calls for service on the ECDC PremierOne Reporting
Service dashboard.
4. Review and input from all personnel in the development of new policies and/or
procedures.
