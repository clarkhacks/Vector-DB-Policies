DEPARTMENTAL GENERAL ORDER
OLIVETTE POLICE DEPARTMENT
OFFICE OF THE CHIEF OF POLICE
Index as:
Immigration Law
ENFORCEMENT OF STATE AND FEDERAL IMMIGRATION LAWS
I. PURPOSE:
The purpose of this General Order to establish guidelines for when Departmental
personnel are contacted by State and Federal Immigration Authorities.
II. PROCEDURES
A. All members of the Department shall cooperate with State and Federal agencies
charged with enforcing immigration laws.
B. Any member of the Department who has contact with a representative of any State or
Federal agency engaged in the enforcement of immigration laws concerning possible
violations of such laws shall immediately notify their on-duty supervisor.
C. This Notification shall be documented in writing by the reporting officer and
supervisor. The supervisor shall make notification through the chain of command to the
Chief of Police who shall issue such further orders as are necessary to effectuate this
policy.
This General Order supersedes all prior directives and orders inconsistent therewith.
