VISION & MISSION STATEMENTS
I. PURPOSE
The purpose of this general order is to establish the values and mission and vision
statements for the Police Department, both external and internal.
II. DEPARTMENT VALUES
INTEGRITY - We are committed to the highest standards of honesty and ethical
conduct, which are the cornerstone of our character.
PROFESSIONALISM - Our conduct and demeanor display the highest standard
of personal and organizational excellence. As a professional organization, we are
guided by the Law Enforcement Code of Ethics.
HONOR - This is a building block in our profession. With reverence for the law,
we pledge to respect human dignity and to have concern for all people.
III. MISSION STATEMENT
The mission of the Olivette Police Department is to maintain a safe City and protect and
serve every person with fairness and compassion while embracing Olivette’s diversity
and honoring the Constitutional rights of all individuals.
IV. VISION STATEMENT
The vision of the Olivette Police Department is to be a regional leader in the delivery of
professional law enforcement services by harnessing the power of community
partnerships, inter-City department relationships, and state-of-the-art resources in order to

V. EXTERNAL STATEMENT
Because we value the citizens of our community, we strive to:
A. Build ongoing relationships of trust and support with the citizens of Olivette,
MO and create a sense of security;
B. Encourage citizen involvement in the development of projects that stabilize
Olivette neighborhoods, improve the quality of life, and proactively diminish
crime;
C. Be ethical, courteous and non-prejudicial in the performance of our duties
without any expectation of personal gain;
D. Always prioritize our response to safeguard human life;
E. Thoroughly investigate any complaint made against members of this
department; and
F. Use response to resistance or aggression only when other reasonable options
have failed.
VI. INTERNAL STATEMENT
The Olivette Police Department strives to provide the highest level of professional law
enforcement service to its citizens. Therefore, the Department is fully committed:

A. To hiring the best qualified officers and employees;
B. To providing extensive and continuous training to all personnel, enabling
employees to achieve successful job performance;
C. To creating an infrastructure in which employee communication will flow
regularly on both vertical and horizontal levels in order to facilitate the goals of
problem-solving policing;
D. To promoting trust, mutual respect, and genuine concern for each employee,
endeavoring to build the same community spirit within the Department as we
endeavor to build with citizens in the community;
E. To encouraging employee autonomy, creativity and innovation in matters that
directly impact their job performance.
F. To managing all human resources efficiently and responsibly; and
G. To maintaining the values and standards we espouse both on and off duty.
This General Order supersedes all prior directives and orders inconsistent therewith.
