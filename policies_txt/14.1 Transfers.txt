TRANSFERS
I. POLICY
When possible, the Olivette Police Department will attempt to fill vacant positions within
the Department, including specialized assignments, through promotion and/or transfer.
Vacancies will be filled without regard to race, creed, religion, disability, color, sex,
national origin, age, marital status, political opinion, sexual orientation, or familial status.
II. SPECIALIZED ASSIGNMENTS
The Department shall post anticipated openings for internal vacant positions and
specialized assignments (non-patrol) throughout the Department and fill positions
through a pre-announced selection process to include:
1. Submission of a memorandum to the Deputy Chief stating relevant experience,
knowledge and abilities or skills;
2. Written recommendation from immediate supervisor and or commander;
3. Final selection and determination by the Chief of Police.
III. ADMINISTRATIVE TRANSFERS
A. Administrative transfers may be made for a variety of reasons, including but not
limited to manpower shortages, light duty, disciplinary actions, job performance, and
expertise.
B. The Chief of Police maintains the authority to assign, reassign, or transfer any member
to such duties or Organizational components as he/she believes best serves the interests
of the member, the Department and the Community.

C. At no time will this policy interfere with the prerogative of the Chief of Police to
transfer permanently or temporarily any member when it is deemed necessary.
Involuntary transfers are not subject to appeal.
V. PATROL TRANSFERS
The Chief of Police will be responsible for the approval of transfer requests. If he/she
approves a request, the information will then be forward to the Deputy Chief to be
processed.
This General Order supersedes all prior directives and orders inconsistent therewith.
